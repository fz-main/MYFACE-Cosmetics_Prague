Process

The Journey

01
Consultation

A detailed in-person or video consultation to understand your goals, review your anatomy and discuss technique, pigment and healing expectations. No obligation, full transparency.

02
Design

Using golden-ratio mapping and digital design tools, we draft your bespoke treatment plan. Colour theory is applied to match your skin undertone and personal palette.

03
Treatment

Each session proceeds with the highest hygiene standards in our fully equipped Prague studio. Topical numbing, premium pigment and calibrated technique ensure comfort and precision.

04
Aftercare

A personalised aftercare kit and digital guide are provided. A complimentary touch-up session is included 6–8 weeks after your initial treatment to perfect the healed result.
